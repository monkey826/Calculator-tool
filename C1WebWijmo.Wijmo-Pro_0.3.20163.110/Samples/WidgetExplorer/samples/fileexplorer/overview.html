Overview

Wijmo FileExplorer widget (wijfileexplorer) completely mimics the Windows Explorer functionality. You can easily add it to your page, organize your files and folders such as creating a new folder, rename, delete or copy a folder, etc.

You can also perform these actions through keyboard, Wijmo FileExplorer widget provides an interface to define custom shortcut keys.

Wijmo FileExplorer widget supports kinds of layout customization, you can configure how to show folder/files, such as with detailed information or just thumbnnail. And you may want to hide some child controls such as the toolbar, addressbox, etc.

If there are too many files to list, you can just show specific folder/files by filtering, or group them by paging.

You will learn these powerful functionalities from other samples in this section.
