Angular 2 component for the DataPoint control.

The wj-flex-chart-data-point component must be contained in one of the following components: WjFlexChartAnnotationText , WjFlexChartAnnotationEllipse , WjFlexChartAnnotationRectangle , WjFlexChartAnnotationLine , WjFlexChartAnnotationPolygon , WjFlexChartAnnotationCircle , WjFlexChartAnnotationSquare or WjFlexChartAnnotationImage.

Use the wj-flex-chart-data-point component to add DataPoint controls to your Angular 2 applications. For details about Angular 2 markup syntax, see Angular 2 Markup.

The WjFlexChartDataPoint component is derived from the DataPoint control and inherits all its properties, events and methods.
